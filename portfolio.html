About Me
Cloud computing means delivering computer services like servers, storage, databases and intelligence, over the internet i.e. the cloud. This allows faster innovation and flexible resources. With the recent shift of companies to cloud-based solutions and start-ups exclusively building on systems like AWS (Amazon Web Services), Cloud Computing is an extremely important skill from which students can benefit. We bring to you a 5-day interactive workshop “CLOUD-NET”, with experts from the Could- Computing field to tell you about the basics and working of it. You’ll be provided with practical experience about this multi-faceted topic. Starting off with AWS and Elastic Beanstalk, the 5-day workshop will leave no page unturned when it comes to Cloud Computing. At the end of the workshop, you’ll have gained a deeper insight into this concept and will be able to apply your knowledge practically.
